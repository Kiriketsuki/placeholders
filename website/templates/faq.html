FAQ

Q1. How do I find house information?

You can retrieve housing information by searching using an address or postal code via the search bar at the top left-hand corner of the map.

Q2. How do I find amenties near an HDB Block?

You can find amenties near an HDB block under the housing information callout.

Q3. There was no search result from the address I keyed in. What should I do?

You can remove "Block" or "Blk" from the address you entered and try searching again. If you are still unable to get a result that means there is no resale HDB flats around the selected area.
